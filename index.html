Questions

Frequently asked questions

What exactly is VasthuWalk?

VasthuWalk is a mobile app that brings traditional Vasthu knowledge to your phone. It gives you the daily Vasthu Naal, detailed guidance for all eight directions of your home, auspicious Grahapravesam dates, Manayadi Sasthiram calculations, and a marketplace of authentic Vasthu products.

Which languages does the app support?

The app is Tamil-first — the guidance, product listings and interface are presented in Tamil so the content stays culturally accurate and easy for families to read together.

What do I get with the Personal Plan (₹196)?

The Personal Plan unlocks full premium access for a 12-hour window, including precise degree calculations and core direction-based Vasthu guidance. It suits homeowners who need answers for a specific decision.

How is the Business Plan (₹499) different?

The Business Plan is valid for a six-month period rather than a single session, making it suited to builders, architects and consultants who need to check guidance repeatedly across projects. After it expires, a new plan or renewal is required.

Are payments secure?

Yes. Subscription and marketplace payments are handled through a secure payment gateway, and your plan activates automatically once payment is confirmed. You can view your full plan and payment history inside the app.

Can I buy Vasthu products through the app?

Yes. The in-app marketplace carries curated items such as statues, yantras and plants. Each order moves through a clear status flow — pending, confirmed, dispatched and delivered — so you always know where your purchase is.

How often are the auspicious dates updated?

Grahapravesam dates and Vasthu Naal information are maintained monthly, so the listings you see in the app stay current without needing an app update.

How do I get help or request account deletion?

Email vasthuwalk@gmail.com or call +91 77888 49994. You can request an update or deletion of your personal information at any time — see our Privacy Policy.
